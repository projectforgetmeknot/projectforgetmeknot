Who We Are

Project Forget Me Knot is a non-profit student-led organization committed to providing high quality emotional support and care for senior residents of the Inland Empire and other underserved communities in California through volunteer-led recreational, musical, and social interaction-oriented service events. We aim to raise awareness and combat mistreatment while also bringing comfort to individuals residing in senior living and assisted care facilities. Our volunteering opportunities are perfect for pre-health and pre-med students! If you are interested in learning more, check out our socials @fmkucr on Instagram for a sneak peek of what we're up to. Drop us a DM or email with any questions and we can't wait to see you at our events!
